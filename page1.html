How to name the different characteristics
These images explain how we call different pileus' and stipes as well what the different spore-bearing surfaces look like. Take a moment to study these images and try to recall what similarities you see from the mushrooms on the previous page. Color is also very important to note because mushrooms of all different colors and shades have the similar pileus' and stipes. Knowing what habitat a given edible mushroom grows in, you can target a good area to hunt for it. For example some mushrooms grow off certain species of trees, whiles others grow off the mulch of leaves. If you collect a sample take its spore print so you can reference it later. After collecting all the discriptive characteristics of a specific sample, you want to refer to an identification book. Identification books are organized to help you use the characteristics you find to guide you to possible mushrooms, elimation ones along the way. On the next pages you will see pictures of mushrooms.
